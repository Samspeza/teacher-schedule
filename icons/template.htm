Logos by Canva

Crafting a unique brand identity starts with a great logo. With Canva, logo-making is easier and more fun. Pick from our vast ready-made logo template collection or create your own. Choose from diverse logo styles—vintage, modern, or minimalist—that best communicate what you are as a brand. Then pick a logo type, either a symbol, icon, wordmark, lettermark, emblem, or a combination, that best connects with your ideal customers and clients. Increase brand recognition by adding a color palette, typography, shapes, and style layouts. Each logo design is optimized at 500 px × 500 px for the highest quality when resizing and printing.

University logos by Canva

In today's competitive academic landscape, a strong first impression is everything. Top-notch professors and groundbreaking research are crucial to your university's reputation, but so is your image. That's where a professional-looking university logo design comes in.

Well-designed logos are a visual representation of your university's core values, history, and academic excellence. It's the symbol that grabs attention on websites, brochures, and social media and tells prospective students, faculty, and donors what your university is all about. However, creating one from scratch is not easy especially if you’re not a graphic design professional. Don’t worry, with Canva’s university logo templates and intuitive editing tools, crafting the perfect university logo is as easy as acing a pop quiz.

Getting started is simple. Browse our library of ready-made logo templates. Whether you’re aiming for a classic collegiate look or a modern academic vibe, our templates cover a wide spectrum of styles to suit every taste and occasion. You can even use a blank university logo template for a more personalized look.

Once you’ve picked a template, you can start customizing the design. Swap out backgrounds like changing class subjects, adjust hues to match your school colors, and insert graphic elements that capture the essence of your institution—all with just a few clicks. If you want to ask for your colleagues’ opinions, just send them the shareable link to your workspace. Now you can all work together at the same time even if you’re not in the same room.

When you’re done, save and download your design in high-resolution JPG, PNG, or PDF. Upload it on your university’s social media pages or incorporate it on your university's official documents–all without leaving Canva.

Why settle for a lackluster logo that doesn’t make the grade? With Canva, you won’t run out of university logo ideas that inspire you and your team to make one. Start designing today.
